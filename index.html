Hello, Traveler!

ActiveTravel Club brings together lovers of active recreation and travel, offering unique itineraries and unforgettable adventures. Our mission is to inspire people to explore the world in unconventional ways, whether it’s hiking through mountain trails, cycling through remote corners of the planet, skiing, diving in crystal-clear waters, or flying in small aircraft. We believe that every journey is an opportunity not just to see new places but also to challenge yourself, expand the boundaries of what’s possible, and immerse yourself in the world of real adventure.

Our team is made up of professionals with years of experience organizing adventure tours, including underwater expeditions and aviation journeys for beginner pilots. We create routes that are suitable for both novices and experienced travelers, always focusing on safety and comfort. ActiveTravel organizes small group trips, fostering an atmosphere of trust and camaraderie, where everyone can feel part of an exciting adventure community. We don’t care about gender, age, or political views—what matters is the spirit of mutual support and cooperation.

By joining ActiveTravel, you’ll gain not only vivid experiences but also new skills such as diving, heli-skiing, or flying an aircraft. With us, every trip is a unique experience!
